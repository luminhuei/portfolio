Hi, I’m Min Huei Lu.

About

Ten years from brand to product — I design the whole journey.

Most recently I was Design & User Experience Officer at Peblla, building restaurant technology used by 750+ restaurants across the US — a handheld POS, self-ordering tablets, a cross-brand loyalty app, and analytics dashboards — contributing to $208M in annual payment volume.

Before that, I spent five years at Gong cha as Lead Designer for Brand & UX/UI — leading a team of 5+ designers through the brand’s US digital journey from zero to launch: app, web, and rewards across 30 stores in California, a 140% increase in US sales, and 7 international design awards along the way.

I started in branding — agency contracts at MOD Worldwide and Iyashi Brand Studio, freelance work for Aetna, CVS, Comcast, and Xfinity — with an MFA in Graphic Design from Academy of Art University. That origin never left me: to me, a design system is just a brand guideline that ships. One language, every surface.

If you’ve read my case studies, you’ll spot the throughline: error-proof beats beautiful, ask about the dining format first, and numbers should read as answers. I work in English and Mandarin — this site speaks both, too.

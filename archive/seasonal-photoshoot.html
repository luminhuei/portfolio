Every season, a new drink becomes a story.

Overview

Art direction and product photography for Gong cha’s seasonal drinks.

Gong cha introduces seasonal drinks every quarter, and every new flavor has to launch across stores, delivery platforms and social media at once. Seasonal product sales typically account for 12–16% of all beverage categories, making each campaign’s visual marketing a high-priority battle.

Working in the brand’s creative studio, I art-directed and produced each season’s full visual asset suite — Key Visuals, product photos, drink SOP training videos and social media creative. One cup of tea telling the same story on in-store posters, delivery banners and Instagram.

Key Visual

One key visual, in-store and online at once.

Each season’s Key Visual serves in-store display and online channels at the same time. For the 2022 Q3 “Fresh Mango” series, we first used Photoshop to piece together three artistic styles and layout directions; once the team collectively settled the direction, photography and extended materials were developed from the established style.

Product Photos

Real ice melts. The story can’t.

Under long exposure to hot lights on set, real ice cubes and slush melt out of shape. We used Photoshop to precisely define each drink’s color and proportion first; on shoot day the food stylist built “photography-only” drinks from props, so every cup held its ideal look in front of the camera.

Social Media

Photos plus motion, a whole season of content.

Building on the approved art direction, photography and motion graphics were combined into a season-long series of social materials — from new-product POP to limited-time campaign visuals, all in one consistent visual language.

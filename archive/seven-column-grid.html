Seven columns — a grid system of my own.

Overview

A meaningful structure that reveals connections.

“7 Column Grid” provides a framework that is demonstrated in my design process. By experimenting with various design methods and research, I’m able to find my own “grid system” — the meaningful structure — that can help to reveal the connections between people, places, things, ideas, and the possible paths to innovation and creativity. I’m fascinated by the process of investigating our world, extracting the most important information, and communicating it to others by displaying it in 7 column grids.

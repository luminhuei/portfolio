Finding focus in a world that won’t sit still.

Overview

A conceptual and typographic system for TYPO SF 2017.

Redesign and expand on campaign material for the 2017 International Design Conference — TYPO Focus. Design a visual concept that demonstrates a conceptual and typographical system.

Shifts in design, society and culture are constant, and designers are forced to make tough decisions. To be competitive, graphic designers need to be competent in web, print, mobile, type design, and UX. In this project, I used a variety of circular design elements to convey the idea of “Focus”.
